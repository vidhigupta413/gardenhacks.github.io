How to make Compost!

1.Select a dry, shady spot near a water source(hose, water pipe)
for your compost pile or bin.
2.Add the materials as they are collected,and make sure larger
pieces are chopped or shredded.
3.Moisten dry materials as they are added.
4.Once your compost pile is established, mix grass clippings and
plant waste into the pile while burying fruit and vegetable
waste under 10 inches of compost material.
What to compost:
Vegetable waste, Fruit scraps, Coffee grounds, Eggshells,
Tea bags, Nut shells, Cardboard,
Paper, Yard trimmings, Grass clippings,
Hay and straw, Leaves, Sawdust, and Wood chips
What not to compost:
Coal or charcoal ash.
Why: Might contain substances harmful to plants
Dairy products, eggs, and meat.
Why: Create odor problems and attract pests such as rodents and flies
Diseased or insect-ridden plants.
Why: Diseases or insects might be transferred back to other plants
Meat or fish bones and scraps.
Why: Create odor problems and attract pests such as rodents and flies
